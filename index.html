Add to Calendar

Tap the button. Calendar will ask you to confirm — that's it.

Part 1 — Copy the link
1
Tap the Copy button on the right to copy the link below.

Part 2 — Open Chrome
2
Find the Chrome app on your phone and open it. Chrome looks like a coloured circle — red, green, yellow and blue. It is not the same as Google Calendar.
3
Tap the address bar at the very top of the screen where web addresses are shown. It may show something like “Google” or a web address. Just tap on it.
4
Delete whatever is there and paste the link you copied in step 1. To paste: press and hold in the address bar for 1 second, then tap Paste when it appears.
5
Tap the blue arrow or press Go on your keyboard to open the link.
6
If you see a Sign in page, log in with your Google account (the same Gmail address linked to your phone). If you are already signed in, this step will be skipped automatically and the calendar page will open directly.
Part 3 — Switch to Desktop view
7
Tap the three dots ⋮ in the top-right corner of Chrome. These are three small dots stacked on top of each other, in the top-right corner.
8
In the menu that appears, tap Desktop site. The page will reload and look smaller — this is normal.
Part 4 — Add the calendar
9
On the left side of the screen, look for Other calendars and tap the + plus sign next to it. You may need to scroll down a little on the left side to find it.
10
Tap From URL in the small menu that appears.
11
Tap the text box that appears and paste the same link again. Press and hold in the box for 1 second, then tap Paste.
12
Tap the Add calendar button. You are done!

✅ The Family Events calendar will now appear in your Google Calendar app automatically. You only need to do this once.

If you get stuck at any step, ask a family member to help you the first time.
